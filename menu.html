DARK CHOCOLATE

Dark chocolate (also known as black chocolate, dark black chocolate, plain chocolate, or sour chocolate) is a form of chocolate containing cocoa solids, cocoa butter, without the milk or butter found in milk chocolate.Government and industry standards of what products may be labeled "Dark chocolate" vary by country and market.

WHITE CHOCOLATE

White chocolate is a chocolate confection, pale ivory in color, made from cocoa butter, sugar, milk solids and sometimes vanilla. White chocolate does not contain cocoa solids, which are found in other types of chocolate,such as milk chocolate and dark chocolate. It is solid at room temperature 25 °C (77 °F) because the melting point of cocoa butter, the only cocoa bean component of white chocolate, is 35 °C (95 °F).

MILK CHOCOLATE

Milk chocolate is a solid chocolate confectionery containing cocoa, sugar and milk. The most popular form of chocolate in many countries, milk chocolate was originally sold solely as a chocolate bar but is now available in a variety of forms. Major milk chocolate producers include Ferrero, Hershey, Mondelez, Mars and Nestlé. Between them, they are responsible for over half of the chocolate sold worldwide.

Amazing Dark Chocolate Brownie With Melting Ice Cream

-Ingredients-

  • 180 g butter
  • 400 g dark chocolate
  • 300 g granulated sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 200 g all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 40 g cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon(s) vanilla extract
Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180ο C (356ο F) set to fan.
  2. Add the 250 g couverture and the butter into a large glass or metal bowl which you place over a saucepan with simmering water.
  3. Stir with a spatula until the chocolate melts.
  4. After the butter and chocolate melt, remove the bowl from the heat and add the sugar inside, by mixing very well.
  5. Then, add into the bowl the eggs one by one by mixing, and add the vanilla too.
  6. Then, add the flour and cocoa powder (sifted) and with a spatula, incorporate them well into the mixture
  7. Finally, add 150 g more chocolate, finely chopped, salt and stir the mixture a little. It would be good for the mixture to be cool so that the chocolate does not melt.
  8. Line a 20x30 cm baking pan (or a 26 cm round springform pan) with parchment paper and cover its bottom and sides.
  9. Butter and flour, with cocoa powder, the parchment paper.
  10. Pour the mixture inside and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  11. Let it cool, cut it into pieces.
    12. For Serving
    • Melted Dark Chocolate.
    • Soft Vanilla Ice Cream.
    • Dust with cocoa powder and serve.
